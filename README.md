# Gilgil Stadium
- Gilgil Stadium area is within Gilgil Municipality.
- Positioned between Nakuru City and Naivasha Town along A104.
- Located at the Juntion between two international trunk Routes.
- The A8 road connecting Mombasa to Malaba, and A4 road, That connects Gilgil to North Horr.


- Gilgil is a National Security Hub, hosting Kenya Army's Kenyatta Barracks, National Youth Service Training College , Anti-stock theft  Unit headquarters, the Kenya Armory and the Administration Police Units.
